Pricing & Approach

How a Typical Visit Works

  1. You share what you need assistance with and any timing preferences.
  2. You receive a clear expectation of time on site and approximate cost.
  3. A visit is scheduled that works with your calendar.
  4. Work is completed, followed by a brief walkthrough and confirmation.

If something falls outside the appropriate scope for a handyman service, that is communicated honestly, along with suggestions for next steps.
